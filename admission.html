🎓 Admission Procedure

Sharda Vidyapith welcomes students from Nursery to Class 10. Our admission process is simple, student-friendly, and ensures a fair opportunity for all.

📌 Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age for Nursery: 3 years
  • Students must meet age and academic criteria for each class
  • Interaction session/interview may be conducted

📝 Required Documents

  • ✅ Birth Certificate
  • ✅ Last Report Card / Transfer Certificate
  • ✅ Aadhaar Card of student & parents
  • ✅ 4 Passport Size Photographs
